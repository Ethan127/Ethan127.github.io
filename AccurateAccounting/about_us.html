History



3111417683_fab03717da, on Flickr

Chartered Accountants, by dbass.ie


Accurate Accounting's story started on December 3rd, 1958. It was founded by Timothy Bean, who visioned easier accounting needs for everyone.

At first, times were hard. Timothy Bean declared bankruptcy in 1964.

However, a wise buyer by the name of John Rodgers bought the company from Timothy. Timothy thought John was crazy.

After years of hard work, however, Timothy and John worked hard and were able to turn a profit from the company.

Now, after 60 years, Accurate Accounting is a successful accounting business that serves many people and companies.


Looking Into the Future

Accurate Accounting celebrated its 50th anniversary on December 3rd, 2008.

Accurate is still growing. We hope that Accurate reaches 100!
